Comfort Food 

With the weather still cold, it’s the perfect time to make way for some nourishing meals that’ll warm you when it's nippy. Grab some lamb joints to get started. From lamb shoulders and legs to ready meals for a hassle-free dinner, you’ll find it all with us. Or how about treating yourself to a hearty slow cooker beef stew? The filling meat paired with juicy dumplings makes for a meal that’s just right for those chilly evenings.   

Cosy night in   

If you’re looking for something soothing, we’ve got you covered with a wide array of fresh soup. Whether you’re a fan of chicken soup or want to dig into a bowl of creamy broccoli and cheddar soup, we’ve got something to suit every palate. Pair it with a tuna pasta bake to make a meal out of it.    

Wintertime calls for cosy nights spent indoors. Once the chill sets in, treat yourself to a steaming cup of hot chocolate. Pop in a couple of marshmallows to take it to the next level. And if you’re settling in for a movie night, a bowl of popcorn is just what you need while enjoying your favourite flicks. From classic butter popcorn to chilli popcorn for that extra kick, you’ll be spoilt for choice. Pair it with a fizzy drink and you’ll be all set.  

If you want something a bit more filling but don’t want to cook, our range of ready meals has got you sorted. Whether you’re craving something Chinese-inspired or want to dig into Indian-inspired meals, we’ve got plenty of options to choose from.
