Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: Oven

Oven cook from chilled. If freezing at home, defrost thoroughly before cooking. Before cooking: Preheat oven to 190°C, Fan 170°C, Gas 5 mark 5. Remove all packaging. Place breast fillets on a baking tray on the middle shelf of the oven for 35 minutes. During cooking: Baste the chicken with the cooking juices. After cooking: Check food is piping hot. All cooking appliances vary. Fillet sizes also vary. This is a guide only.

Warnings:

Don't wash raw chicken.

Keep raw poultry separate from cooked foods.

Use separate kitchen utensils and surfaces for raw poultry and cooked foods or wash them thoroughly in between use.

Wash hands after handling raw poultry.

Cook raw poultry thoroughly until it is piping hot throughout and no pink colour remains.

Poultry is cooked if the juices run clear when the deepest part of the flesh is pierced with a thin skewer.
